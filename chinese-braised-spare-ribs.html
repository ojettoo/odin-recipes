Chinese Braised Spare Ribs

Chinese Spare Ribs are an excellent addition to any mealtime. Although they can be a little rich, they are a guaranteed hit for any meat-lover's table.

These tasty ribs will go fantastically with rice and some stir-fried veg. So get your utensils at the ready, and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork spareribs, cut into 3 inch pieces
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, sliced
  • 5 sliced green onions
  • Half tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil ribs in a large pot of water for 3 to 5 minutes. Drain and set aside
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Throw in ginger, green onions, cinnamon and heat until the flavour fills your nose.
  3. Throw in spareribs, cook for about 5 minutes until golden on both sides.
  4. Add water, soy sauce, sugar and rice wine. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to a low simmer for about one hour.
  5. Enjoy!

And there you have it, a fantastically sweet and fragrant meat dish for the whole family!
