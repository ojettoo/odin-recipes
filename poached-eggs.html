Poached Eggs

Poached Eggs are a delicious addition to any breakfast table. In my humble opinion, a well cooked egg white, complemented by a runny yolk in the center, on top of a lightly toasted English muffin is one of the best ways to start the day!

Ingredients

    1 tsp champagne vinegar
    1/2 tsp salt
    2 fresh eggs

Instructions

  1. Fill a bowl with water.
  2. Boil a saucepan of water, adding salt and the vinegar. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Crack your eggs into the simmering water. Make sure not to make a mess, try to crack slowly and close to the water so the egg doesn't spread out too far.
  4. After about 5 minutes, scoop out the eggs with a slotted spoon and place them in the ice water. Reheat for 1 minute.
  5. Do your best to drain the poached eggs of excess water and then place on top of a buttered muffin. Enjoy!
