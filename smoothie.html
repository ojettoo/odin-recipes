Daily Smoothie

This smoothie is your daily dose of essential vitamins. Have one every morning with breakfast to start the day with a burst of healthy energy! Feel free to add whey protein for an additional protein boost, or other seasonal fruits to complement these three staple fruits!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole banana
  • Half an apple
  • A handful of bluberries
  • Half a cup of water
  • Any other seasonal fruits to go with these

Instructions

  1. Begin by cutting the apple and banana into small chunks and throwing them into a smoothie maker.
  2. Next, add the blueberries, water and any other fruits or whey protein.
  3. Blend for a minute and a half.
  4. Enjoy!
